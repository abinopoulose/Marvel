COMPANY PROFILE

Marvel International is a Sharjah based Trading Company actively involved in Import, Export & Trading of special building materials indispensable for today’s construction industry. Our varied product range finds its application in almost all civil construction projects.

OUR MISSION

Marvel's mission is to be a leading business house in UAE driven by innovative products, ethical practices & professional management. The Company will strive for total customer satisfaction by offering quality products and efficient service through its dedicated staff, with a vision to be the market leader in areas of its core competence by 2010.



Marvel Intl. has been blessed with excellent business relationships with some of the best companies & partners in the world and have an excellent team of dedicated professionals who are all engaged in our quest for Quality Excellence, not just in what we do but also in how we do it. We are confident to meet our client’s needs of quality building materials at affordable price levels.
